Rice Platters

Original Platter

Indulge in a Mediterranean journey with our Original Platter. This dish combines fragrant rice with marinated meats, roasted veggies, and authentic Mediterranean spices. It's a flavorful escape on your plate.

Key Ingredients: Seasoned rice, sliced shawarma meat, garlic sauce, and a side of fresh salad.

Deluxe Platter

The Deluxe Platter is the epitome of rice perfection. Enjoy a lavish spread of aromatic rice, succulent meats, crisp veggies, and a secret sauce that elevates this dish to greatness. Every bite is a taste of supreme delight.

Key Ingredients: Seasoned rice, sliced shawarma meat, tahini sauce, grilled vegetables, and a side of fresh salad.

Shawarma Feast

Savor the Shawarma Feast, a harmonious blend of spicy flavors, tender meats, and fresh vegetables. This dish is a fiery celebration of taste and texture, perfect for those who crave a spicy kick in their meal.

Key Ingredients: Seasoned rice, a variety of shawarma meats, tahini sauce, grilled vegetables, and a side of fresh salad.
